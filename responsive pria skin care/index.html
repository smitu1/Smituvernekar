PRODUCT FEATURES

Niacinamide , also known as Nicotinamide is a form of vitaminB3

Niacinamide is a water-soluble vitamin that plays a crucial role in maintaining healthy skin, among other bodily functions. In skincare, niacinamide is highly valued for its ability to address a variety of skin concerns due to its versatile and gentle properties.

  • Controls Oil Production: For those with oily or combination skin, niacinamide can help control excess sebum, preventing breakouts and shine without drying out the skin.

  • Minimizes Pore Appearance: Regular use of niacinamide can help to regulate sebum production and tighten pores, leading to smoother skin.

Vitamin C serum ia a skincare product formulated with ascorbic acid

Vitamin C is a potent brightening agent. It inhibits melanin production, which helps reduce the appearance of dark spots, hyperpigmentation, and sun damage. Over time, it can give the skin a more radiant, even complexion.It’s commonly used to brighten the skin, protect it from environmental damage, and support the production of collagen, which helps maintain skin elasticity and firmness.

  • Vitamin C is known to support the skin’s natural repair processes. It can help with post-acne scarring, promote wound healing, and improve the overall texture of the skin.

  • Vitamin C boosts collagen production, which helps improve skin elasticity.
